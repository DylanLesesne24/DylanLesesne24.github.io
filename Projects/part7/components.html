Components

Every computer is made up of core components that work together to bring your system to life. Here’s a breakdown of the major parts of a PC and their roles.

CPU (Processor)

The CPU is the brain of the computer. It handles instructions and processes information.

Examples: Intel i5, Intel i7, AMD Ryzen 5, AMD Ryzen 7

GPU (Graphics Card)

The GPU renders images, videos, and games. It’s essential for gaming and creative work.

Examples: NVIDIA RTX 3060, RTX 3070, AMD RX 6700 XT

RAM (Memory)

RAM is your computer’s short-term memory, allowing quick access to active data and tasks.

Examples: 8GB DDR4, 16GB DDR4, 32GB DDR5

Motherboard

The motherboard connects all your components together and provides power and communication pathways.

Examples: ATX, Micro-ATX, Mini-ITX boards

Storage

Storage devices hold your files, operating system, and software. SSDs are faster than HDDs.

Examples: 500GB SSD, 1TB SSD, 2TB HDD

Power Supply (PSU)

The power supply unit delivers electricity to all your components safely and efficiently.

Examples: 500W, 650W, 750W
